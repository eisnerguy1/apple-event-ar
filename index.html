For the past few years, Apple has hidden an AR easter egg on their Apple Event page for the most upcoming event.

The Apple logo will launch the Apple Event pages (via The Internet Archive), the MacRumors/9to5Mac icons will launch the article showcasing the easter eggs. Clicking on the event banners will launch the AR easter egg itself.

To launch the WWDC22 AR easter egg, click the event banner to get randomly 1 of 26 different card packs. You'll need to reload this page to get a different random pack.
